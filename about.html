NewsPlay practice creativity in covering the news

  • back to the game

NewsPlay is a game to generate creative approaches to covering news stories. It's like Iron Chef, but for news. Can you cook the news ingredients with a creative method?

What are the ingredients?

A summary of a recent article story from the NY Times (Article Search API).

What are the remix options?

A collection of new constraints to present the story.

How to play?

On loading the site, you will be given the ingredients and the options. Use the next 5 minutes to develop an approach to that story that you could pitch to a publication editor for a publication of your choice.

Draft a 3- to 5-sentence pitch. If you feel inspired by the supplemental media type or the platform, add your ideas for additional social media content in your pitch.

Once you've got a good idea, submit your approach at the bottom of the page.
